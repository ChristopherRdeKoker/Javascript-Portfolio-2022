MY TIMELINE

2019 - 2021

4IR SETA Proposal Facilitator- Blue Elevator

Having returned from a long trip abroad, I was presented an opportunity to facilitate and develop 4IR(4th Industrial Revolution) proposals to UIF & NSF SETA's. This was an integrative role, with many individuals working on many different facets of these proposals to host on-site agriculture experience, business-orientated skills, and basic computer training to underskilled youths in the community.

2017 - 2019

Foreign English Tutor- American Eagle - Chengdu

Having performed well in my initial role as English teacher/tutor, I decided to continue with the company American Eagle, and transferred to a main branch in Chengdu, where I continued in my position whilst also taking on more administrative and volunteer related work, including hosting seasonal events for the company.

2015 - 2017

Foreign English Tutor- American Eagle - Nanjing

An opportunity presented itself to me, to travel overseas to China and earn money to repay student loans after completing a Bachelors and Honours degree. My time at this company was really pleasant, with many extra-curriculum activities and administrative duties attached to this role.

2013 - 2014

Assistant Manager/Administrator- K.D.T - Beach View Bnb

Having completed Honours in General Psychology, and having returned from my first 9months abroad, I worked as an assistant to two individuals on the South Coast, working primarily in administrative work, as well as advertising & marketing for their respective companies.

2012

Kwazulu Natal University- Honours Degree in General Psychology

Having completed my initial degree in General & Industrial Psychology, I had the opportunity to further extent my studies and competency. With hard-work and many sleepless nights, obtained my Honours in General Psychology. During this time, I also volunteered at a local Primary school in Glenwood, Durban, which helps accommodate both physically and mentally handicapped children. My role was to help in many different areas such as private tutoring, help coach handicapped basketball teams, and introduced basic computer lessons for the older grades.

2009 - 2011

Kwazulu Natal University- Bachelors Degree in General & Industrial Psychology

I've completed my Bachelors degree in General & Industrial Psychology, from Howard campus, University of Kwazulu Natal. For the first year, I was placed top 3, and was invited to join the Golden Key Society due to my accomplishments. It was around this time that I also took an interest in Computer Sciences related courses for exra credit.
